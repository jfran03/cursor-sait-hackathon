How does the nudge reach the phone / watch during the demo?

This is the "wow moment" — judges see a real device light up. The question is what actually fires the notification.

A

Push notification via Expo / React Native

A minimal companion app on your phone receives a push from the Halo backend. Shows up as a native iOS/Android notification — appears on phone and auto-forwards to Apple Watch / Wear OS. Real device, real OS notification, zero theatrics.

B

SMS via Twilio

Halo texts the nudge to your number. Zero app install. Judges see a real iMessage bubble land on the phone mid-demo. Forwards to Apple Watch natively. Fastest to wire up — Twilio free tier covers it.

C

Web Push (PWA)

Halo is installed as a PWA on the phone. The backend sends a Web Push notification — appears on the lock screen and watch. No app store, no native app. Works on Android natively; iOS 17+ supports it.

D

Simulated (phone on screen, no real device)

Phone mockup rendered in the browser alongside the dashboard. Animated notification card slides in on cue. No real device — but fully controlled, zero risk of demo failure, and judges still see "what it would look like."
