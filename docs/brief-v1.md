## Prompt

---

## Judging Criteria

| Criterion | Weight |
|---|---|
| Innovation | 25% |
| Technical Execution | 20% |
| Functional Completeness | 20% |
| Problem-Solution Fit | 20% |
| UX | 5% |
| Demo | 5% |
| Ambition | 5% |

**Takeaway:** Innovation + Problem-Solution Fit = 45% of your score. The idea and its fit matter more than anything else. Technical execution and completeness together are 40% — it needs to actually work.
